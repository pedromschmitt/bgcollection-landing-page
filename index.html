Questions and Answers

  • What is the Board Game Collection system?

    It is a system that we developed at our board game store after five years of experience renting boardgames.

    We saw that controlling the rental dates using spreadsheets caused many failures and errors. Sometimes a rental was late, and we did not realize it, or no one knew which team member had received the rental back from the costumer.

    In addition, customers never knew if a game was rented or had already been returned, and they had to call the store all the time.

    We tested various systems and generic rental programs, but none worked very well for board games.

    So we created our own! And after others shopkeepers asked to use our system, it turned into Board Game Collection.

    We included all the specific needs of a boardgames store. For example, the registration for your board games pulls the data from the BGG: just enter the game ID that the system will register with the image, the number of players, the average game time, and other data.

    If you have your collection at BGG, we can import everything for you, with no additional cost, just request information by email or chat.

  • Can I cancel anytime?

    Yes, the platform has no subscription terms. Just send an email and you are done! Account canceled.

  • If I test the site and then want cancel my account, can I export my data?

    Yes! All data is exportable to the excel csv / spreadsheet format. Board games, customers, subscriber plans (if your store has subscription plans), and rentals.

  • When I create my account, do I get an internet site address?

    Yes, as soon as your store creates an account in the Game Collection, you will receive a site with an exclusive subdomain, chosen by you, like 'griffo.ourboardgamecollection.com'. There is no additional cost.

    This is the address that your customers will use to view your collection and you will use it to manage your rentals.
